About Us

Laravel Kerala is a group of Laravel developers who are passionate about promoting and enhancing Laravel development in Kerala, India. We organize events, meetups, and workshops to share knowledge and foster a strong Laravel community.

At Laravel Kerala, we conduct regular events and meetups to bring Laravel enthusiasts together. Keep an eye on our upcoming events to participate and expand your Laravel skills and network.
